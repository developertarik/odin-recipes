Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Description

Shrimp scampi with pasta is the ultimate seafood pasta dish. Works with any pasta; angel hair is less filling.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Shallots and garlic
  • White wine
  • Lemon juice
  • Seasonings
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Have all of your ingredients prepared and ready to cook before heating your skillet.
  2. Boil the pasta in well-salted water, drain, and hold aside before cooking the shrimp scampi.
  3. It takes just a couple of minutes to cook raw shrimp, and if they cook for too long, shrimp turn tough and rubbery.
  4. Before you drain the pasta, hold aside a half cup of the pasta water to add to the sauce if the finished dish looks a little dry
